Menu

Drinks

Coffee

Espresso

A strong and concentrated coffee made by forcing a small amount of nearly boiling water through finely-ground coffee beans.

Cappuccino

Espresso mixed with steamed milk and topped with a layer of frothed milk, often dusted with cocoa powder or cinnamon.

Latte

Espresso with a generous amount of steamed milk, resulting in a creamy and smooth coffee with a mild espresso flavor.

Mocha

Espresso mixed with hot chocolate, steamed milk, and topped with whipped cream. A perfect blend of coffee and chocolate.

Americano

Espresso shots diluted with hot water, resulting in a coffee that's similar in strength to regular drip coffee.

Tea

Green Tea

A light and refreshing tea made from unoxidized leaves, known for its grassy and vegetal flavor.

Earl Grey

Black tea flavored with bergamot oil, creating a citrusy and aromatic tea with a slightly sweet undertone.

Chamomile Tea

A caffeine-free herbal tea made from chamomile flowers, known for its calming and soothing properties.

Oolong Tea

A partially oxidized tea with a range of flavors, from floral and fruity to rich and roasted, depending on the variety.

Peppermint Tea

A herbal tea made from peppermint leaves, offering a refreshing and minty flavor, perfect for relaxation.

Spirits

Whiskey Sour

A cocktail made with whiskey, lemon juice, and simple syrup, typically served with a maraschino cherry and a slice of orange.

Martini

An iconic cocktail made with gin or vodka, often served in a chilled glass and garnished with an olive or lemon twist.

Mojito

A Cuban highball cocktail made with white rum, sugar, lime juice, mint leaves, and soda water, perfect for a tropical escape.

Old Fashioned

A classic cocktail made with bourbon or rye whiskey, sugar, and bitters, often garnished with a citrus twist and a cherry.
