Our Story

Peace on Earth was created in 2018 by two sisters and their dad, with a vision of creating a unique space in Provo that would make people feel welcomed, loved, and appreciated. provo needed something new and fresh- and that’s just what we provide. Beyond creating delicious food and drinks, peace on earth provides a community space where local art, music, poetry and creatives of all types are welcome and celebrated.

Our Mission

Our commitment goes beyond crafting delectable food and beverages; we are architects of a community space where local art, music, poetry, and the diverse creative voices of our community converge and thrive. We celebrate the unique talents and passions of every soul who graces our doors. Peace on Earth is more than a coffee shop; it is a sanctuary for camaraderie, a canvas for expression, and a sanctuary where the spirit of unity blossoms.
With every sip of our coffee, bite of our pastries, and gathering of kindred spirits, we strive to nourish not only the body but also the soul. At Peace on Earth, we are not just brewing coffee; we are brewing connections, fostering artistry, and cultivating a space where love, acceptance, and creativity flourish. Join us on this heartfelt journey, and together, let us bring peace, harmony, and a fresh sense of community to Provo.

Doing Our Part

We go the extra mile to ensure that the ingredients and products we use are not just of the highest quality, but also sourced with integrity. Our journey takes us to local farms and artisanal suppliers who share our passion for sustainable and ethical practices. We believe in supporting local communities and reducing our carbon footprint.
From the coffee beans that journey from farmer to cup with fairness and transparency, to the fresh, locally-sourced ingredients that grace our menu, every choice we make is a conscious one. We are committed to fostering positive relationships with our suppliers and ensuring that they are treated with the same respect and care that we extend to our cherished guests.
When you enjoy a cup of coffee or a bite to eat at Peace on Earth, you can savor not only the rich flavors but also the knowledge that your choice supports ethical sourcing practices, empowers local producers, and contributes to a more sustainable and compassionate world. Join us in savoring the taste of ethical goodness in every sip and every bite.
